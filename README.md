# 📊 Sales Forecasting & Demand Analysis

![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-yellow.svg)

## 🚀 Overview
This project focuses on analyzing historical sales data to identify trends and forecast future demand.  
An interactive dashboard was built using **Power BI** to support data-driven planning and decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze historical sales patterns  
- Identify regional sales performance  
- Understand monthly sales trends  
- Evaluate product sub-category performance  
- Support demand forecasting and inventory planning  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI  
- Microsoft Excel  
- Data Cleaning & Transformation  
- Data Visualization  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- 💰 Total Sales: **4.52M**  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 🌍 Regional Analysis
- **West region** generates the highest sales contribution (~2M+)  
- Followed by East, Central, and South regions  
- Indicates strong regional imbalance → opportunity for expansion in low-performing regions  

---

### 📅 Monthly Trends
- Sales show fluctuations across months with peaks around:
  - **September**
  - **November**
- Lower sales observed in early months (January–February)  
- Suggests seasonal demand patterns  

---

### 🛍️ Sub-Category Performance
- **Phones** dominate sales significantly (~2M+)  
- Other categories like Chairs, Storage, and Tables contribute moderately  
- Indicates high demand for technology-related products  

---

### 📊 Trend Analysis
- Sales trend shows gradual growth with intermittent dips  
- Strong upward trend in later months indicates increasing demand
